Turn static receipts into a premium, interactive presentation layer people can actually read and explore.

This application solves a common product problem: receipts and proof-of-payment artifacts usually feel disposable, unreadable, and visually weak. MAXCHICHAR turns them into a polished, physics-driven surface that can be showcased, inspected, and personalized.

What It Solves
If you are building checkout flows, digital storefronts, dashboards, or proof-of-purchase experiences, this gives you a stronger presentation layer. Instead of showing a flat screenshot, the receipt becomes a readable hero object with motion, depth, and interaction.
